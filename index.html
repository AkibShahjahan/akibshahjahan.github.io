Senndat

Note-Sharing Web Application Platform

Senndat is a platform for university students to study in a collaborative and engaging way. Initially we are focussed on creating the best platform for taking notes by providing a very elegant text editor. The purpose of this Web App is for students to share their knowledge with their fellow class mates, while taking great notes for themselves in their very own virtual “Notebook”. This it to stimulate collaborative learning in way never seen before.

We used the MEAN stack (Mongo, ExpressJS, AngularJS, NodeJS) for the development of this app, along with Facebook Authentication. My partner and I divided up the work in vertical slices so that we could experience working with all the technologies.

Troventi

Peer-To-Peer Marketplace Mobile Startup

Troventi is an online peer-to-peer platform for buying and selling used textbooks. We connect trusted buyers directly with reliable sellers through our custom real-time marketplace. Users will be able to buy or sell books they need through our database as well as negotiate prices and schedule meet-ups to complete transactions using our efficient navigation system. Sellers can easily set prices through our recommendations and quickly find buyers for their books. We process payments, offering buyers the option of paying by cash or securely with their credit card. Troventi is the simplest, cheapest, most secure and convenient way to buy and sell textbooks.

I am the main iOS Developer for this startup. I worked on the frontend logic as well as created all the required screens for the app. Apart from the basic frontend logic API call integration, I also worked on user authentication, phone number verification system and the GPS system.

Check out Troventi's website here, like and share our Facebook page here, and stay tuned for the big launch.

Bastobe

Mobile Social App

This app will revolutionize how we stay informed about our community and how we view the world. The mission of this app is to help people live the world. It’s for helping people to see the world around them at the moment and share their own piece of the world.

Users will be able to take pictures and videos and share them based on their current location. The medias will last for 24hours before disappearing. A live view of the local and global community will be manifested by these medias for people to get an understanding of the "scene" around them and also around the world. The posts will include options for liking, commenting and sharing. The app includes a dynamic feed page ranked based on time and popularity. The app also has a map feature for viewing any part of the world. The camera the app provides, is versatile and includes similar modern features as that of FB Messenger and Snapchat’s cameras.

This is the main project I have been working on for the last several months.. The version 1 for iOS is completed and is up on the app store here.

The Android version is currently in development.
